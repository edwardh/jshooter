JShooter Help

Controls: Mouse (left and right) - move rocket
Mouse left click - fire single shot
Mouse right click - fire double shot
To toggle sound on and off, use the checkbox in the top right of the screen.
Your rocket ship. You have 3 lives initially. It can fire energy blasts, but whenever you fire you deplete your energy (shown at the top left of the screen, along with how many lives you have). Your energy regenerates slowly.
A pack alien. He appears in a pack of other such aliens. He can drop bombs. If a bomb or an pack alien hits you, you lose a life. Each level has progressively more pack aliens. Sometimes a pack alien will break from his formation and fly down to attack you.
A crab alien. Crab aliens appear in packs and fly in a circular formation. They can drop bombs. Crab aliens are worth more points than pack aliens.
A tank alien. Tank aliens appear by themselves and bounce around dropping bombs. They are fast and take 3 hits to kill. They are worth more points than crab or pack aliens.
A shield. It will protect you from aliens and their bombs. It can take one hit before it blows up.
An asteroid. Generally it will just get in your way, but if it hits you, you'll lost a life. If you shoot it it will split into two smaller asteroids.
A life power up. If you catch it, it will give you an extra life. You can have a maximum of five lives.
An energy power up. If you catch it, it will completely replenish your energy.
